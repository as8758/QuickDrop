QuickDrop Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Overview

QuickDrop is a browser extension that helps you fill online forms faster by storing your information locally in your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

QuickDrop collects and stores the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone number)
  • Address information
  • Education history
  • Work experience
  • Professional links (LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio)
  • Any custom fields you choose to add

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API (chrome.storage.local). Your information:

  • Never leaves your browser
  • Is not transmitted to any external servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Remains under your complete control

Data Usage

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Populate form fields when you drag and drop or click to paste
  • Display your saved information in the extension interface
  • Provide right-click context menu options for quick insertion

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally in your browser, it is protected by your browser's security mechanisms. We do not have access to your data at any time.

Third-Party Access

QuickDrop does not:

  • Collect analytics or usage data
  • Use tracking cookies
  • Share data with third parties
  • Sell user information
  • Transmit data over the internet

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the extension (removes all stored data)
  • Clearing your browser's extension data
  • Editing your profile through the setup page

Permissions Explained

  • storage: Store your form data locally in your browser
  • activeTab: Insert data into form fields on the current webpage
  • contextMenus: Provide right-click insertion options
  • scripting: Enable drag-and-drop functionality
  • sidePanel: Display the extension interface
  • host permissions: Work on all websites you visit

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.
